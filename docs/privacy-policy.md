# Privacy Policy for Twist

**Effective Date:** 2026-06-19

This Privacy Policy describes how Twist ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our iOS application.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

**Twist does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers.**

All processing is performed entirely on your device. Specifically:

- **Photos / Screenshot Images** — When you select a Spotify screenshot, the image is processed locally using Apple's Vision framework for text recognition (OCR). The image and its contents are never uploaded or shared with us.
- **Apple Music Library** — The app requests access to your Apple Music library solely to create playlists. No library data is transmitted to us.

---

## 2. Third-Party Services

Twist uses the following third-party SDK:

### Google AdMob
The app displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google may collect certain information for ad delivery. For children using this app, AdMob is configured to serve **non-personalized, COPPA-compliant ads only**. No behavioral tracking or interest-based advertising data is collected from children.

- [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [Google Families Policy](https://support.google.com/googleplay/android-developer/answer/9893335)

---

## 3. Children's Privacy

Twist is designed to be safe for users of all ages, including children under 13.

- We do **not** collect personal information from children.
- We do **not** share any data with third parties for behavioral advertising targeting children.
- Ads displayed to users in the Kids category are non-personalized and comply with the [Apple App Store Kids Category guidelines](https://developer.apple.com/app-store/review/guidelines/#kids) and COPPA requirements.

---

## 4. Data Storage

All data handled by Twist remains on your device. We do not maintain any servers or databases that store user information.

---

| Apple Music | Create a playlist in your Apple Music library |
